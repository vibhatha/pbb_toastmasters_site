Professionals Beyond Boundaries Toastmasters Club (PBBTMC), based in Sri Lanka, is a fully virtual Toastmasters club established for enthusiastic professionals who believe in and value continuous learning and self-growth while becoming more effective communicators and leaders.

Our members come from various professions and reside in different regions of the country and the world, yet they share a common futuristic orientation toward developing themselves and others.

We chartered on 1st February 2022 and since then, Professionals Beyond Boundaries Toastmasters has grown into a vibrant community where diverse voices come together to inspire, support, and challenge one another.

At PBBTMC, we believe that the journey of self-development is limitless. Our club's goal is to empower professionals to unlock their potential, step beyond their comfort zones, and embrace every opportunity to grow as communicators and leaders. With each meeting, we strive to uphold the Toastmasters values of integrity, respect, service, and excellence, while embracing our own theme of Beyond Boundaries, encouraging members to push past their limits and achieve greatness.
